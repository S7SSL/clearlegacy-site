Can I get a will written the same day in the UK?

England & Wales · Urgent Wills

Quick answer

Yes — same-day is genuinely possible because a UK will is valid the instant it is correctly signed and witnessed, with no waiting period or registration. You complete a guided online will, print it, and sign it with two independent witnesses the same day. ClearLegacy lets you complete the online step in one sitting; the only real constraint is arranging witnesses.

Detailed explanation

Same-day works for straightforward estates. Here's the realistic path:

  1. Complete the online will (often 15–30 minutes).
  2. Print it the same day.
  3. Sign with two independent witnesses present together — valid immediately.

If your affairs are complex, a same-day simple will gives immediate cover, with a solicitor review to follow for anything intricate.

Example scenario

Needing cover before a same-week procedure, Nadia completes her will online in the morning and signs it that afternoon with two colleagues — a valid will the same day.

A note on “service” claims: a will's validity is same-day by law. Any specific turnaround promise should be one the provider actually offers — check the provider's stated process before relying on a fixed timescale.
