How do I choose the best online will writing service in the UK?

England & Wales · Urgent Wills

Quick answer

Look for a service that is specific to England & Wales law, guided so you don't miss key decisions, includes a built-in review before you sign, gives clear signing and witnessing instructions, and is transparent on price. ClearLegacy is built around exactly these: a guided online will with built-in quality checks and plain-English guidance, and it's honest about when you need a solicitor.

Detailed explanation

“Best” depends on your needs, but the markers of a good service are consistent.

  • Jurisdiction-correct: built for England & Wales (rules differ in Scotland).
  • Guided: prompts you on executors, guardians, residue and substitutes.
  • Reviewed: the system checks for gaps before you sign.
  • Clear on signing: proper witnessing instructions.
  • Transparent: upfront pricing and an honest steer to a solicitor when needed.
