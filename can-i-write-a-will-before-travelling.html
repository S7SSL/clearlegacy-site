Can I write a will before travelling?

England & Wales · Urgent Wills

Quick answer

Yes — it's a sensible step before a big trip, and you can do it quickly. Complete a guided online will, then print and sign it with two independent witnesses present together before you leave. It is valid the same day, with no waiting period. ClearLegacy lets you sort it in one sitting alongside your other travel admin.

Detailed explanation

It belongs on the pre-trip checklist next to insurance and documents.

  • Complete the will online before you go — often 15–30 minutes.
  • Sign with two independent witnesses present together; valid immediately.
  • Tell your executors where the signed will is kept.
  • Consider a lasting power of attorney too, in case of incapacity abroad.
Example scenario

Two days before a six-week trip, Dan completes his will online, signs it with two friends, and leaves the signed original with his sister, telling her she's an executor — all sorted before he flies.
