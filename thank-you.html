Next steps — signing your Will

  1. Print the PDF.
  2. Ask two witnesses to be with you at the same time. They must be 18+, of sound mind, and must not be named as beneficiaries (nor married to anyone who is).
  3. Sign and date the Will on the last page in the presence of both witnesses.
  4. Each witness signs and prints their details in your presence and in the presence of each other.
  5. Store the signed original somewhere safe and tell your executor(s) where it is.
