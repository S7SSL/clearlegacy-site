Certificate Provider & Preferences

An LPA requires a "certificate provider" to confirm you understand the document and are not being pressured to sign it.

Your certificate provider must be someone who has known you for at least 2 years OR a professional such as a solicitor, GP or social worker. They cannot be related to you or your attorneys.
