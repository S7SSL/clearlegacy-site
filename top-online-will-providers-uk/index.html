Who shouldn't use a top online will provider?

Honest answer: anyone with a genuinely complex estate. Online will services — including ClearLegacy — are built for standard UK estates. If any of the following apply, a high-street solicitor is the better choice:

  • Discretionary trusts or life-interest trusts
  • Foreign assets or non-UK domicile questions
  • Business succession with multiple shareholders
  • Estranged spouses or contested family circumstances
  • Estates likely to exceed £2m where the residence nil-rate band tapers

For everyone else — the majority of UK adults with straightforward estates — a top online will provider is the right product. The only question is which one fits your priorities, which is what this ranking is for.
