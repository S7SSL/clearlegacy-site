How quickly can I write a will online?

England & Wales · Urgent Wills

Quick answer

For a straightforward estate, writing a will online often takes 15–30 minutes — sometimes less. With ClearLegacy you answer guided questions, then print and sign. Because a will is valid as soon as it is correctly signed and witnessed, you can go from starting to a legally valid will the same day. Complex estates take longer and may need a solicitor.

Detailed explanation

The writing is quick; the part that makes it valid is the signing.

  • Simple estate: often 15–30 minutes to complete the questionnaire.
  • Print and sign: with two independent witnesses present together.
  • Valid immediately on correct signing — no waiting period.
  • Gather your details first (assets, executors, beneficiaries) to go faster.
Example scenario

Tom gathers his details over a cup of tea, completes the ClearLegacy questionnaire in about 20 minutes, and signs the printed will with two colleagues the next morning — valid straight away.
