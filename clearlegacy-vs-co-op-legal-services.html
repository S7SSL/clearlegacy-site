FAQs

How much cheaper is ClearLegacy than Co-op?

Considerably, for a standard will. ClearLegacy is £69 single / £99 mirror. Co-op Legal Services starts from £150 for a single will and £245 for mirror wills (inc VAT). For a straightforward will the legal result is the same; you're paying Co-op for solicitor involvement.

Is Co-op better because it uses solicitors?

Solicitor involvement is valuable for complex estates or if you want personal advice. For a straightforward will, a correctly made online will is equally valid. Choose Co-op if you want that reassurance and will pay for it; choose ClearLegacy for speed and price.

Can ClearLegacy handle a trust will?

ClearLegacy is built for straightforward wills. If you need a trust will, we'll flag that your situation needs specialist advice before you start — in which case a solicitor-backed service like Co-op, or a private-client solicitor, is the right route.
