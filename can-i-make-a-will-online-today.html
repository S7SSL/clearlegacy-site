Can I make a will online today in the UK?

England & Wales · Urgent Wills

Quick answer

Yes. You can write a will online today and it becomes legally valid the moment it is correctly signed and witnessed — there is no waiting period or registration step in England and Wales. Services like ClearLegacy let you complete the questionnaire online in one sitting; you then print it and sign it with two independent witnesses present together to make it valid.

Detailed explanation

Nothing in law forces you to wait. Validity depends on how the will is made and signed, not on time elapsed.

  • Complete a guided online will (ClearLegacy walks you through the decisions).
  • Print it — an electronic-only or unsigned will is not yet valid in England & Wales.
  • Sign it in front of two independent witnesses (not beneficiaries), present at the same time.
  • It is valid from that moment — the same day you started.
Example scenario

Facing surgery tomorrow, Priya completes the ClearLegacy questionnaire this afternoon, prints her will that evening, and signs it with two neighbours as witnesses. Her will is legally valid that same night.
