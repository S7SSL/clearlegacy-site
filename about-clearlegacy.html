About ClearLegacy

England & Wales · About &middot; ClearLegacy

Quick answer

ClearLegacy is an online will-writing and estate-planning service for England & Wales. We help people make a valid will through a guided questionnaire with a human review step, and we publish plain-English guidance, scenarios and tools — including a free Estate Risk Assessment — so families can plan with confidence and avoid the intestacy default.

Who we are and what we do

ClearLegacy exists to make estate planning clear, accessible and affordable. Too many people put off making a will because it feels complicated, expensive or grim. We break it into simple, guided steps.

What we offer

  • Online will writing — a guided questionnaire that produces a valid England & Wales will, with a human check before you sign.
  • A free Estate Risk Assessment — a short tool that flags the gaps most likely to cause disputes, delay or tax.
  • A large, sourced knowledge base — clear answers, real-world scenarios, decision trees and UK statistics, all reviewed by our editorial team.

Our editorial standards

Our guidance is written and reviewed by the ClearLegacy editorial team, kept current with the law of England and Wales, and cited to primary sources such as legislation and GOV.UK. We are clear about one thing throughout: our content is general legal information, not personal legal advice — for advice on your situation, speak to a qualified solicitor or STEP practitioner.

Who we help

Homeowners, parents, unmarried couples, people in second marriages, executors, and anyone who wants their wishes recorded properly rather than left to the statutory rules.

Why it matters

Around 59% of UK adults have no will. Without one, the intestacy rules decide who inherits — often excluding unmarried partners and stepchildren entirely. ClearLegacy helps people replace that default with their own clear instructions.

Plain promise: we give clear information and a simple way to act on it. We never pretend to replace tailored legal advice for complex estates — we tell you when to seek it.
What happens next?
  1. Complete the questionnaireA few guided questions about you, your family and your wishes.
  2. Human reviewYour answers are checked by the ClearLegacy editorial team for completeness.
  3. Receive your documentsYour will and supporting paperwork are produced, ready to print.
  4. Sign correctlyClear instructions on signing and witnessing so the will is legally valid.
  5. Protect your familyYour wishes are recorded and your loved ones are spared the intestacy default.
