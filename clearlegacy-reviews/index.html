Three things customers comment on most

1. Speed

The single most common feedback theme. Most customers receive their will within 24 hours; many report under 12 hours when they submit during the working week. Customers contrast this directly with solicitor turnaround times of 2–4 weeks.

2. Price

Customers regularly mention quoted solicitor prices in the £300–£500 range and contrast with the £69 ClearLegacy fee. The recurring comment is "same legal document, fraction of the price."

3. Clarity of signing instructions

Customers consistently note that the signing instructions that arrive with the PDF are clear and practical — what counts as an independent witness, how to sit when signing, what to do with the original afterwards. This is where DIY kits fail most often, and it shows up positively in our feedback.

How we handle negative feedback

When a customer is unhappy with their will, we reply by email and offer the free amendment under the policy. Drafting issues are corrected without charge. Process issues (timing, communications) are reviewed and the relevant team is briefed. The negative reviews on third-party platforms remain visible — we don't ask for them to be taken down.
